POE Ninja build price checker

Important notes:
  • To check the price of a build, a poe.ninja build tab must be active.
  • The tool doesn't use sockets, sockets colors and links on the search.
  • If no result was found, you can click on the trade link and try to change the values or deselect a mod. (Keep in mind that clicking on another tab will close the extension).
  • CSV download is enabled after loading the items. The CSV contains all the links to the poe trade.
